A question is LLM-related if it concerns developing with, using, deploying, or troubleshooting large language models or LLM-based tools/APIs (e.g. ChatGPT, GPT-x, Claude, Gemini, LangChain, RAG pipelines, embeddings for LLMs). Questions about classical ML, reinforcement learning (e.g. OpenAI Gym), rule-based chatbots, or unrelated uses of matching words (Gemini the zodiac/exchange, people named Claude) are NOT LLM-related.
